YOUTUBE CONTROL CENTER - WEB STORE DESCRIPTION

TAGLINE:
The ultimate extension to enhance and control your YouTube experience with high-quality video, player optimizations, and a distraction-free layout.

DESCRIPTION:
Take full control of your YouTube experience with Youtube Control Center. Tired of low-resolution videos, distracting Shorts, or auto-dubbed audio? This powerful, lightweight extension is designed to give you the most premium, high-quality, and personalized viewing experience possible.

Whether you are a cinephile wanting the highest bitrate or a casual viewer looking to declutter your feed, Youtube Control Center has you covered.

KEY FEATURES:

* Super Resolution & Quality Lock: Never settle for blurry videos. Set your preferred resolution (up to 4K/8K) and ensure you always get the highest quality available instantly.

* Enhanced Bitrate: Unlock the best possible visual fidelity by prioritizing premium bitrate streams. Enjoy sharper details and smoother motion on every video.

* Advanced Player Layouts: Switch between Cinema Mode and Default view automatically. Our Auto-Resize feature optimizes the player layout for your screen size.

* Force Original Audio: Stop YouTube from automatically playing dubbed versions of videos. Always hear the original audio track exactly as the creator intended.

* Sticky Video Player: Keep the video in view even while you scroll! The Sticky Video feature fixes the player at the top while allowing you to browse the sidebar videos list effortlessly.

* Hide YouTube Shorts: Declutter your feed by removing Shorts shelves and tabs. Focus on the content you love without vertical distractions.

* Modern UI with Dark Mode: A stunning, premium interface that fits perfectly with modern web design. Toggle between Light and Dark modes to suit your viewing environment.

WHY CHOOSE YOUTUBE CONTROL CENTER?

* Performance First: Built with modern web technologies to ensure zero lag and minimal resource usage.

* Privacy Focused: We respect your privacy. No data tracking, no analytics, just pure functionality.

* Intuitive Design: A simple, beautiful popup menu allows you to toggle features on and off with ease.
